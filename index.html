About Us

Meet Raven and Kim!

Raven and Kim are college students of Pamantasan ng Cabuyao currently taking Bachelor of Sciences in Information Technology and Bachelor of Sciences in Electronics Engineering. By combining their passion for creativity and their drive to support their education financially, Raven and Kim have created a business that not only provides a unique and thoughtful gift option but also empowers young entrepreneurs to pursue their dreams.

Our Mission

Craft with RK is dedicated to creating unique and high-quality crochet flower arrangements that are perfect for any occasion, from weddings to birthdays to just brightening up someone's day. Their mission is to inspire creativity and bring a smile to people's faces with their beautiful, handmade creations.
